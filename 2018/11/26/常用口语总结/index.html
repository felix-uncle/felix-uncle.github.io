Posted on 2018-11-26

旅游英语（Travel English）

1. Directions(问路)

　Do you know where the hotel is?
　
　Do you know where the beach is?
　
　It’s on the right.
　
　It’s on the left.
　
　It’s straight ahead.
　
　It’s on the corner.
　
　It’s around the corner.
　
　It’s 3 blocks ahead.
　
　around the corner on your right.(右边👉的拐角处)
　
　Where can I find a grocery store?(我在哪可以找到杂货店？)
　
　Can you recommend a good restaurant nearby?
　
　Where Can I go to eat some authentic local food?
　(请问我在哪可以吃到地道的本地菜呢？)

2. Money

　
　How do I get to the bank?
　
　Excuse me, do you know where an ATM is?
　
　Do you know where I can exchange some money? 　

3. Food

　Hi, I’d like a table for 2.(你好，我想要一个两人的桌位)
　
　Hi, I reserved a table for 3.
　
　Could I see the menu please?
　
　What are the specials today?
　
　What dish can you recommend?
　
　Could I get the bill please?(请把账单给我好吗？) 　

4.Meeting people (交盆友)

　
　Hello，How‘s it going?
　
　Where are you guys from?
　
　Have you done much sight seeing yet?
　
　What’s been the best part of your trip? 　

5.Shopping

　
　May I try this on first?
　
　Where are the changing rooms?(请问更衣室在哪里？)
　
　It’s too expensive for me, Can you come down a little bit on the price?
　
　Is this your best price?(这是你给出的最好的价格吗？)
　
　Can I pay by credit card? 　

6.hotel

　
　I’d like to make a reservation.
　
　I’d like a single room.
　
　I’d like to book a double room for 3 nights.
　
　How much is a double room per night?
　
　What time is check-in?(旅店什么时候办理入住？)
　
　What time is check-out? (旅店什么时候退房？)
